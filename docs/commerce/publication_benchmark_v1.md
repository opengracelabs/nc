# Publication Benchmark v1.0

This benchmark defines the strategic publication and channel deployment rules for the core Nature & Culture asset fixtures. It codifies the "Go-to-Market" logic for the **Publishing Service**.

---

## 1. Channel & Audience Definitions

### Primary Channels
*   **Shopify (Owned):** The "Museum Store" experience. High-margin, brand-building, and premium collections.
*   **Etsy (Marketplace):** High-velocity discovery. Targeted at "Vintage Decor" and "Gift" seekers.
*   **Pinterest/Instagram (Social):** Visual discovery and "Top of Funnel" awareness.

### Target Audiences
*   **The Collector:** Seeks "Masterwork" archival prints and rare historical documents.
*   **The Decorator:** Seeks "Flagship" aesthetics for modern home/office interiors.
*   **The Educator:** Seeks "Standard/Reference" assets for scientific and historical context.
*   **The Traveler:** Seeks "Heritage" keepsakes from iconic National Parks.

## 3. Detailed Publication Benchmarks

### 3.1 Thomas Moran: *The Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone*
*   **Primary Channel:** Shopify (Exclusive Premium Collection).
*   **Target Audience:** High-net-worth collectors and landscape art enthusiasts.
*   **Timing:** March 1st (Yellowstone Anniversary).
*   **Priority:** 100/100 (The "Halo" Asset).
*   **Strategy:** Launch with a dedicated long-form narrative landing page and "Limit 100" numbered archival prints.

### 3.2 1871 Hayden Survey Map (Yellowstone)
*   **Primary Channel:** Shopify / High-end Etsy.
*   **Target Audience:** Cartography collectors and "Deep History" buffs.
*   **Timing:** Mid-April (National Park Week).
*   **Priority:** 95/100.
*   **Strategy:** Position as the "Foundational Blueprint." Cross-sell with Moran prints as a "Discovery Duo."

### 3.3 William Henry Jackson: *Old Faithful Geyser*
*   **Primary Channel:** Etsy (High-volume discovery).
*   **Target Audience:** National Park fans and vintage photography seekers.
*   **Timing:** June-August (Summer peak tourism).
*   **Priority:** 85/100.
*   **Strategy:** Utilize as a "Gateway" asset for low-barrier products (Postcards, Calendars) to drive traffic to Shopify.

### 3.4 John James Audubon: *Wildlife Series*
*   **Primary Channel:** Etsy (Core Marketplace) / Shopify (Curated Sets).
*   **Target Audience:** General wildlife enthusiasts and "Farmhouse/Traditional" decorators.
*   **Timing:** September (National Wildlife Day) / Seasonal Floral.
*   **Priority:** 90/100.
*   **Strategy:** High-velocity Etsy SEO play using "Audubon Print" as the primary keyword anchor.

### 3.5 Ernst Haeckel: *Scientific Art Series*
*   **Primary Channel:** Shopify / Instagram-First.
*   **Target Audience:** Interior designers and "New Aesthetic" / Art Nouveau seekers.
*   **Timing:** January-February (Home refresh season).
*   **Priority:** 80/100.
*   **Strategy:** Heavy social media focus on geometric symmetry and home decor tiling potential.
