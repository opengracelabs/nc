# Execution Benchmark v1.0

This benchmark provides the final "Go/No-Go" criteria for the commercial launch of the Nature & Culture asset fixtures. It codifies launch readiness, release risk, and the mandatory curator gates required before public deployment.

---

## 1. Execution Readiness Tiers

*   **READY:** All metadata, print-ready files, and product mappings are verified. No further action required.
*   **NEEDS_RESTORATION:** Asset requires digital cleaning (noise removal, color correction) to meet "Museum Grade" standards.
*   **NEEDS_METADATA:** Missing critical historical, scientific, or institutional provenance data.
*   **BLOCKED:** Fails rights verification or visual quality hard gates.

## 3. Detailed Execution Benchmarks

### 3.1 Thomas Moran: *The Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone*
*   **Execution Strategy:** "The Halo Launch." The centerpiece of the Yellowstone Heritage Collection.
*   **Readiness Detail:** High-resolution TIFF (600 DPI) verified.
*   **Release Risk:** **LOW** (Technical). Risk is limited to "Grand Scale" (40x60") print clarity.
*   **Curator Gate:** Mandatory visual verification of the 40x60" proofing derivative.
*   **Priority:** 100/100.

### 3.2 1871 Hayden Survey Map (Yellowstone)
*   **Execution Strategy:** "The Foundation Series." Establishing historical and scientific authority.
*   **Readiness Detail:** **NEEDS_METADATA**. Requires full transcription of expedition surveyor notes and original legend text.
*   **Release Risk:** **LOW** (Technical). Risk is limited to fine-line technical legibility of survey markers.
*   **Curator Gate:** Mandatory verification of historical metadata and transcription accuracy.
*   **Priority:** 95/100.

### 3.3 William Henry Jackson: *Old Faithful Geyser*
*   **Execution Strategy:** "The Heritage Keepsake." Capturing travel nostalgia and historical proof.
*   **Readiness Detail:** **NEEDS_RESTORATION**. Requires digital removal of 19th-century "Archive Noise" (foxing, chemical staining, and minor edge tears).
*   **Release Risk:** **MEDIUM** (Market). Risk is B&W photography's performance against high-color fine art in an "Impulse Buy" environment.
*   **Curator Gate:** Optional review of the restoration quality and sepia-tone balance.
*   **Priority:** 85/100.

### 3.4 John James Audubon: *Wildlife Series* (Bison & Wolf)
*   **Execution Strategy:** "The Marketplace Engine." High-volume Etsy and Shopify deployment.
*   **Readiness Detail:** **READY**. All plates restored and metadata tagged (DwC/Taxon).
*   **Release Risk:** **LOW** (Brand). Risk is market saturation of generic Audubon prints; mitigated by our "Museum Grade" provenance and restoration quality.
*   **Curator Gate:** Optional standard QA check.
*   **Priority:** 90/100.

### 3.5 Ernst Haeckel: *Scientific Art Series*
*   **Execution Strategy:** "The Aesthetic Niche." Targeting designers and geometric art enthusiasts.
*   **Readiness Detail:** **READY**. All symmetrical plates verified for pattern tiling.
*   **Release Risk:** **LOW** (Technical). Risk is limited to the precision of geometric centering in POD templates.
*   **Curator Gate:** Optional check for tiling/symmetry alignment in home decor mockups.
*   **Priority:** 80/100.
