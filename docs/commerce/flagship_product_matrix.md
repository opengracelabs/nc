# Nature & Culture Flagship Product Family Matrix

This matrix provides strategic product recommendations for the 70 flagship places in the Nature & Culture portfolio, acting as a guide for commerce development and curation.

### 1. Kyoto (Washoku)
**Hero Product Family:** Home Decor
**Secondary Product Family:** Books
**Third Product Family:** Wall Art
**Reasoning:** High-end kitchenware and seasonal wellness products align with Washoku's aesthetic precision, while Edo-period food prints from the Smithsonian offer exceptional wall art and culinary book content.
