# Yellowstone Launch Flow: Proof & Vision

**Asset Zero:** William Henry Jackson, *Old Faithful Stereoview* (1871)  
**Asset One:** Thomas Moran, *Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone* (1872)

---

## 1. Homepage Flow: The Threshold of Wonder

**Goal:** Establish Nature & Culture as an authoritative portal of discovery.

1.  **The Entry (Hero):** 
    *   **Visual:** Edge-to-edge, slow-motion video of Old Faithful erupting at dawn.
    *   **Overlay:** "Nature & Culture: The World, Curated."
    *   **Behavior:** A subtle "Trust Band" at the bottom shows the logos of the **British Library**, **Smithsonian**, and **Library of Congress**.
2.  **The Featured Expedition:**
    *   **Component:** "The 1871 Hayden Expedition: Proving the Impossible."
    *   **Layout:** A side-by-side comparison. Jackson's black-and-white *Asset Zero* (The Proof) next to Moran's vibrant *Asset One* (The Vision).
    *   **CTA:** "Walk the Frontier."

---

## 2. Place Page Flow: The Spatial Story

**Goal:** Weave the spatial, historical, and artistic narratives into a single thread.

1.  **The Spatial Anchor:** The page opens with **Henry Elliott’s 1871 Route Map**. A pulsing indicator marks the expedition's first major camp.
2.  **Scroll Point 1: The Witness (Asset Zero):** 
    *   **Visual:** Jackson’s stereoview of Old Faithful slides into view over the map.
    *   **Narrative:** "September 1871: The geysers were rumors until Jackson captured this light. This photograph provided the undeniable proof required by Congress."
    *   **Atmosphere:** Thermal bubbling audio at 30% volume.
3.  **Scroll Point 2: The Sublime (Asset One):**
    *   **Visual:** As the user continues to scroll, the map fades, and Moran's chromolithograph of the Grand Canyon expands to fill the screen.
    *   **Narrative:** "Moran translated Jackson's data into a national icon. His vibrant colors revealed the soul of the wilderness."
    *   **Atmosphere:** Wind audio at 50% volume; UI luminescence shifts to "Sulfur Yellow."

---

## 3. Media Viewer Flow: The Infinite Zoom

**Goal:** Provide an unmediated, tactile connection to the original assets.

### Media View: Asset Zero (Jackson Photography)
*   **Transition:** A "Zero-UI" fade. All text and navigation disappear.
*   **Interaction:** High-inertia zoom into the silver gelatin grain. 
*   **Close Look Hotspots:** 
    *   **"The Glass Plate":** Explains the 1871 photographic process.
    *   **"Geyserite":** A scientific annotation on the mineral deposits visible in the foreground.

### Media View: Asset One (Moran Fine Art)
*   **Transition:** Screen luminosity shifts to "Golden Hour."
*   **Interaction:** Infinite Zoom into the lithographic stippling.
*   **Close Look Hotspots:** 
    *   **"Sulfur & Fire":** Links the yellows in the painting to the biological datasets (Thermus aquaticus).
    *   **"Art as Activism":** Tells the story of how this specific print was shown to Congress to save the park.

---

## 4. Experience Summary: Wonder, Trust, Connection

| Phase | Strategy | Experience Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Homepage** | The Threshold | **Trust:** Established via institutional logos and Jackson's proof. |
| **Place Page** | The Story | **Connection:** User follows the expedition from map to photo to art. |
| **Media Viewer** | The Infinite Zoom | **Wonder:** Unmediated access to the microscopic soul of the assets. |
