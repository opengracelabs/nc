# 10 Institution Expansion Roadmap: Portfolio v2

**Role:** Chief Curator  
**Objective:** Define a 10-institution roadmap to achieve near-complete global coverage, categorized by Tier A (Platform) and Tier B (Flagship) priorities.

---

## 1. Executive Summary

This roadmap prioritizes institutions that provide either the **Global Scaffolding** (Metadata/Spatial) or the **Visual Masterpieces** (High-Res CC0) required to deliver a world-class experience. The selection surgically addresses regional gaps in Japan, Australia, and the Global South.

---

## 5. Implementation Sequence

### Phase 1: The Authority Layer (Q3 2026)
*   **Integrate UNEP-WCMC & Wikidata:** Ground every flagship in authoritative spatial and semantic metadata.
*   **Getty CC0 Ingestion:** Launch high-resolution Yosemite and Machu Picchu journeys.

### Phase 2: The Atmospheric Layer (Q4 2026)
*   **Kyoto Univ. & British Library:** Deploy the "Sacred Stillness" (Kyoto) and "Soundscape" (Global) updates.
*   **Trove (NLA):** Build the foundational marine record for the Great Barrier Reef.

### Phase 3: The Deep-Curation Layer (Q1 2027)
*   **Kew & EAP:** Enrich the biological and indigenous narratives for Galápagos and the Global South.
*   **UNESCO 3D:** Implement the "Spatial UI" for world heritage sites.
