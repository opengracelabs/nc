## 1. Why it should be first

Thomas Moran’s chromolithograph is the **"Genesis Asset"** of the National Park movement. Historically, it was this specific visual proof that convinced the US Congress to establish Yellowstone as the world’s first National Park. Strategically, it is the most multimodal asset in the collection, capable of anchoring the visual, historical, and commercial narratives simultaneously.
