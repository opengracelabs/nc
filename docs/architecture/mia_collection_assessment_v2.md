## 1. Executive Summary: The Rights-First Realignment
This v2 assessment incorporates a critical realignment of Mia’s integration feasibility. While Mia’s metadata is universally CC0, its **media rights are object-level**. The high-fidelity, commercially usable public-domain subset is confirmed at ~50,000 objects (approx. 55% of the collection). This is a significant reduction from the initial "universally open" assumption but remains a world-class Tier 1 opportunity for Nature & Culture.

**Revised Recommendation:** **TIER 1 (Strategic Authority)**
