## 2. Strategic Evaluation

### 2.1 Earth Imagery & Climate Archives
*   **The Ultimate Anchor:** The *Blue Marble* and *Earthrise* are the most influential "Nature" images in history.
*   **Scientific Witness:** Decades of Landsat and Earth Observatory data provide the high-fidelity scaffolding for scrollytelling journeys on deforestation, glacier retreat, and urban expansion.
*   **Aesthetic Detail:** Satellite imagery (e.g., "Ocean Sands, Bahamas") provides abstract, painterly natural patterns of high commercial and aesthetic value.

### 2.2 Space Exploration & Human Heritage
*   **Apollo Legacy:** The definitive visual archive of human ingenuity and "Discovery" culture.
*   **Interplanetary Discovery:** High-res surface data from Mars (Perseverance/Curiosity), Saturn (Cassini), and Pluto (New Horizons) expands the definition of "Nature" to a galactic scale.

### 2.3 Astronomy & Deep Space
*   **Visual Awe:** Hubble and James Webb (JWST) imagery represents the pinnacle of "Deep Time" photography.
*   **Cultural Connection:** Constellations and nebulae link ancient human mythology with modern scientific verification.

It is the essential scientific and aesthetic balance to the fine-art focused Tier 1s.
