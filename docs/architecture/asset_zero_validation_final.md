## 3. Final Recommendation: Procedural Shift

While Moran's masterpiece is the "Visual Soul" of the platform, **William Henry Jackson’s 1871 Photograph of Old Faithful in Eruption (Europeana PDM)** should be **Asset Zero**.

### Rationale for the Shift:

1.  **Trust as the Primary Directive:** As a discovery platform, Nature & Culture must first establish that it is a source of **undeniable truth**. Jackson's photograph is the literal record of the "moment of discovery." Starting with art (Moran) risks framing the platform as a gallery; starting with photography (Jackson) frames it as an **authoritative archive**.
2.  **Live Europeana Validation:** The Europeana Public Domain Mark (PDM) photograph is a "cleaner" technical validation of the Ingestion Worker. It is a primary capture, whereas Moran's chromolithograph is a 19th-century derivative (reproduction).
3.  **The "Scientific Witness":** Yellowstone was dismissed as a myth until Jackson’s photos arrived. By making his photo Asset Zero, we honor the **foundational proof** that allowed the park to exist in the first place.

### The Strategy: "Proof then Vision"
*   **Asset 0:** **W.H. Jackson Photograph (The Proof).** Validates Truth, Trust, and Technical Substrate.
*   **Asset 1:** **Thomas Moran Chromolithograph (The Vision).** Validates Wonder, Atmosphere, and Commerce.
