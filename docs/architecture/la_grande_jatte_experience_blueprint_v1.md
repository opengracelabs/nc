# La Grande Jatte Experience Blueprint v1: Luminous Leisure

**Asset:** *A Sunday on La Grande Jatte — 1884* (1884–86)  
**Artwork ID:** 27992  
**Institution:** Art Institute of Chicago (AIC)  
**Focus:** France · Paris · Seine · Pointillism · Impressionism

---

## 1. Wonder: The Homepage Hero ("The Luminous Moment")

**Goal:** Capture the visitor's imagination through the optical science of the "Floating Dot."

*   **Experience:** Full-bleed, immersive entry. A custom **"Pointillist Shimmer" WebGL shader** animates the individual dots of color, creating a breathing, kinetic field of light.
*   **Audio:** A "Seurat Soundscape"—the distant rustle of a 1880s Sunday, mixed with the hypnotic, repetitive rhythm of a Satie-inspired piano track.
*   **Interaction:** A "Prism View" toggle that allows users to see the painting "deconstruct" into its primary color components (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Black) before reforming.

## 2. Trust: The Institutional Authority

**Goal:** Validate the asset's heritage and ensure scientific/historical integrity.

*   **Metadata Entry:** A digital replica of the **Helen Birch Bartlett Memorial Collection** accession file (1926), establishing the provenance and AIC's stewardship.
*   **Institutional Seal:** The **AIC "European Painting and Sculpture" department** verified badge, linked directly to the IIIF Presentation API manifest.
*   **Conservation Layer:** An optional "X-Ray" toggle revealing the underlying pencil sketches and Seurat's initial compositional grid.

## 3. Connection: The Place Page ("A Stroll on the Seine")

**Goal:** Anchor the masterpiece in the physical reality of Paris and the River Seine.

*   **Geographic Anchor:** **Île de la Grande Jatte**, Asnières-sur-Seine.
*   **Interactive Cartography:** A "Time-Slider Map" allowing visitors to toggle between a 1880s survey of the island and a modern-day satellite view, highlighting the specific spots where Seurat set his easel.
*   **The Flâneur's Guide:** A narrative journey through the "Leisure Revolution" of 19th-century Paris, linking the painting to the social history of the Seine.
*   **Atmospheric Link:** Integration with a "Parisian Sky" engine that adjusts the site's ambient light to match the current time of day in Paris.

## 4. Discovery: The Media Viewer ("The Pointillist Lens")

**Goal:** Enable deep, scientific exploration of Seurat's revolutionary technique.

*   **Interaction:** 2000% IIIF Deep-Zoom capability.
*   **Guided Hotspots:**
    *   **The Law of Contrast:** Highlighting the juxtaposition of complementary colors.
    *   **The Painted Frame:** Explaining Seurat's radical decision to include the frame in the optical composition.
    *   **The Monkey's Secret:** A social/satirical layer exploring the symbolism of the exotic animals in the park.

## 5. Commerce: The Parisian Boutique ("Atelier Seurat")

**Goal:** Materialize the experience into high-value physical patronage.

*   **Curated Collection:**
    *   **Masterwork Canvas Prints:** Museum-grade giclée reproductions on textured canvas.
    *   **"Sunday Afternoon" Scent:** A sensory companion (Scent: River Water, Cotton, Crushed Grass).
    *   **Pointillist Color Kits:** Educational tools and prisms for exploring color theory at home.
*   **CTA:** "Your patronage supports the Art Institute of Chicago's mission to share the world's masterpieces."
