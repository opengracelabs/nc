## 1. Executive Summary
The Minneapolis Institute of Art (Mia) is a world-class "encyclopedic" institution that excels in the digital domain. It is a leader in the **Open Access** movement, providing over 50,000 CC0 images and a pioneering collection of **350+ CC0 3D models**. Mia provides essential cultural anchors for Asian and African art that are often siloed or restricted in other Tier 1 institutions.

**Recommendation:** **TIER 1 (High Priority)**  
**Prioritization Score:** **92/100**

## 2. Strategic Evaluation

### 2.1 Cultural Breadth & Geographic Diversity
*   **East Asian Anchor:** World-class Japanese (*kacho-ga*) and Chinese (bronzes/jades) collections. Fills the Asia-Pacific gap with "Elite" status.
*   **African Heritage:** The *Ife Shrine Head* and Benin Bronze plaques provide deep, high-authority anchors for West African history.
*   **Midwest/Plains America:** Essential holdings of Native American (Plains) beadwork and textiles, grounding the American heartland narrative.

### 2.2 Nature & Culture Storytelling Value
*   **Scientific Fantasy:** Japanese bird-and-flower prints (*kacho-ga*) by Hokusai and Hiroshige perfectly bridge biological accuracy with artistic spirit.
*   **Geothermal/Climate:** Van Gogh’s *Olive Trees* and Monet’s *Grainstacks* anchor "Atmospheric Runtime" narratives on light, heat, and landscape resilience.
*   **Tactile Archaeology:** 3D scans of ritual vessels (e.g., the Owl 'Zun') allow for "Volumetric Discovery" of ancient human-nature relationships.

### 2.3 Tourism & Product Potential
*   **Tourism Journeys:** "The Silk Road in Bronze," "Van Gogh’s Provence," and "The Royal City of Ife."
*   **Print-on-Demand (POD):** High velocity for Japanese prints (Koson, Rakusan) and Mellon’s *Sudarium* (single-line engraving).
*   **AR/VR:** Ready-to-use CC0 3D models for mobile discovery experiences and digital souvenirs.

## 5. Recommendation Summary

**Mia should be prioritized as a Tier 1 source.**

**Rationale:**
Mia offers the "Tactile Authority" that Nature & Culture currently lacks. While the Met and Getty provide massive 2D depth, Mia’s **3D CC0 pipeline** is the only production-ready source for high-fidelity volumetric assets. Furthermore, its **Japanese bird-and-flower** collection is more accessible and surgically curated for nature-centered discovery than Hokusai holdings at the Met.

**Prioritization Score: 92/100**
*   **Rights (25/25):** CC0 1.0 Universal throughout core collection.
*   **Visuals (23/25):** Exceptional high-res masters and 3D scans.
*   **Tech (24/25):** Developer-native data (GitHub/JSON) and Sketchfab API.
*   **Uniqueness (20/25):** Fills the Ife/Yoruba and high-fidelity Japanese botanical gaps.
