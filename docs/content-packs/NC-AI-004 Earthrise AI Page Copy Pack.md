## 2. Earthrise Story Page Copy
**Headline:** The Oasis in Space.
**Subhead:** The story of the photograph that launched a movement.

**The Discovery:**
In December 1968, humanity left Earth for the Moon. While the mission's focus was the lunar surface, its most enduring legacy was a view looking back. Astronaut William Anders, looking out the window of the Apollo 8 spacecraft, saw the Earth rising—a vibrant, cloud-streaked sphere against the absolute black of space.

**The Impact:**
"Oh my God! Look at that picture over there!" Anders famously exclaimed. This unplanned moment became *Earthrise*, a visual testament to our planet’s isolation and fragility. It provided the emotional and scientific catalyst for the modern environmental movement, reminding us that we all live on a single, shared island in the cosmic desert.
