## 2. Collection Narrative: The Oasis in the Desert
On Christmas Eve, 1968, the crew of Apollo 8 emerged from the lunar far side to witness a miracle rising over the horizon. This was not the stark, cratered desert they had been sent to map. It was a vibrant, cloud-streaked sphere of life, suspended in an infinite void.

The *Earthrise Collection* is a curated repository of this transition. It maps the cognitive shift from "conquering space" to "protecting home." Every artifact in this collection—from our remastered Heritage Prints to our scientific Discovery Folios—is grounded in the original NASA Ektachrome frames. We return to the primary records of William Anders’ Hasselblad camera to provide the definitive visual account of the moment the modern environmental movement was born. 

This is more than a gallery; it is a testament to the "Overview Effect"—the profound understanding that we live on a lonely, beautiful island in the cosmic desert, and we are its only protectors.
