## Governing Method

250 places in five cohorts of 50. Each cohort has a primary optimization target.
No place in this list duplicates the 98 unique places in NC-PLACES-001.

**Composite score (0–100):** Heritage (0–30) + PD (0–40) + Gap (0–20) + Commerce (0–10).
Same model as NC-PLACES-FACTORY-001. Within each cohort, places are ranked by score.

**Gap codes:** `GEO` geographic zero-fill · `ICH` cultural/ICH activation · `ECO` ecosystem zero-fill · `DES` designation activation · `COL` collection strength · `FAM` expedition/family anchor

**Type codes:** NC-PLACES-1000 taxonomy. `N.PK.NP` = National Park · `M.MX.NC` = Mixed WH · `N.GE.GP` = Geopark · `X.DS.RV` = Dark Sky Reserve · `C.HU.HC` = Historic City · etc.

**Designation abbreviations:** WH-N = UNESCO WH Natural · WH-C = UNESCO WH Cultural · WH-M = UNESCO WH Mixed · BS = Biosphere Reserve · GP = UNESCO Geopark · RS = Ramsar · MPA = Marine Protected Area · DS = Dark Sky · II = IUCN Category II
