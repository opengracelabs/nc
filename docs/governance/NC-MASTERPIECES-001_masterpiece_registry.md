## Governing Principle

A Masterpiece is not merely NC's best-quality illustration. It is an IO whose combination of cultural weight, scientific significance, rights clarity, and commercial potential makes it an anchor — for a collection, for a discovery journey, for an educational narrative, and for NC's identity as an institution.

**The Masterpiece designation carries five obligations:**
1. The work must eventually have a full 7-link NC-PDPS provenance chain
2. The work drives the CC0/Family A (Museum Giclée) tier wherever rights permit
3. The Masterpiece earns a reserved slot in the CE Allocation Register
4. Every Masterpiece must have, eventually, all three editorial stories: Illustrator · Place · Expedition/Discovery
5. Masterpiece status is governed. It is not a marketing label. It is an institutional declaration.

## 1. Masterpiece Scoring Model

Six dimensions, each scored 1–10 (max 60). NC-IO-001's five dimensions (IA, RC, PS, IQ, ND) are subsumed by this Masterpiece-specific six-criteria framework.

| Criterion | Code | Governs | Max |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Cultural Significance** | CS | Is this work known outside specialist circles? Does it appear in histories of art, science, or exploration? Is it reproducible in NC's brand story? | 10 |
| **Scientific Significance** | SS | Did this illustration advance or document scientific understanding? Does it record species, phenomena, or discoveries of lasting importance? | 10 |
| **Discovery Graph Value** | DG | How many collection triggers, place anchors, expedition threads, and discovery journeys does this IO generate simultaneously? | 10 |
| **Educational Value** | EV | Can this IO anchor a curriculum module? Does it have cross-disciplinary appeal (biology + art history + geography + ecology)? | 10 |
| **Product Value** | PV | Is this IO viable for Family A (Museum Giclée)? CE-worthy? Does it perform commercially across multiple product families? | 10 |
| **Rights Confidence** | RC | How certain is the PD/CC0 determination? Is the contributing institution's DD ratified? Is the image at the resolution required for Family A? | 10 |

**RC scoring for this document:**

| Rights Situation | RC Score |
|---|---|
| CC0 institution-wide, DD ratified + image ≥6000px confirmed | **10** |
| CC0 per-record, DD ratified or drafted + image quality confirmed | **9** |
| Pre-1928 PD + BHL Tier 1 CC0 member, DD drafted | **9** |
| Smithsonian CC0 (pending DD-SMITHSONIAN-001) | **8** |
| MNHN/Kew/SPRI (no DD, known PD, ToS unconfirmed) | **7** |
| Tokyo NM / non-Western institution (no DD, rights uncertain) | **6** |

**Masterpiece threshold:** Score ≥ 55/60 → Top 100 Masterpiece candidate.
**Elite IO threshold:** Score ≥ 45/60 → Top 1,000 portfolio.

## 5. Masterpiece Designation Governance

### Designation Rules

**MG-1:** Masterpiece designation is permanent. A Masterpiece is not demoted to Elite IO except on discovery of a rights defect (RC = 0.000 blocks all commercial activation but does not remove the Masterpiece designation — the work retains its cultural standing).

**MG-2:** Masterpiece score is calculated at designation time using the best available rights information. As DDs are ratified and RC scores improve, the Masterpiece score may be revised upward but not downward except on rights defect discovery.

**MG-3:** The Masterpiece Registry is capped at 100. To add a new Masterpiece, one existing entry must be retired (not deleted — marked as SUPERSEDED). Retirement requires a formal DD-level review.

**MG-4:** The Extinction Archive is a permanent editorial priority. Any illustration of a species subsequently confirmed extinct automatically qualifies for Masterpiece designation review.

**MG-5:** Every Masterpiece must eventually have all three editorial stories: Illustrator biography · Place story · Expedition/discovery record. Missing stories are open actions, not blockers.

**MG-6:** Every Masterpiece is automatically eligible for Family A (Museum Giclée) once its source institution's DD is ratified and image quality ≥6000px is confirmed. The Museum Giclée is not automatically activated — it still requires curator approval and Gate E.

**MG-7:** CE editions for Masterpieces are governed by the CE Allocation Register (NC-IO-001 §9). Masterpiece status does not bypass allocation limits. It does, however, grant priority in CE queue when allocation opens.
