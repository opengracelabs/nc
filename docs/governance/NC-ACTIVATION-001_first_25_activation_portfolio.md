## Selection Methodology

Six criteria evaluated for each candidate. No single criterion eliminates a place — all six
contribute to the final rank. Where a lower-scoring place closes a regional zero not covered
by any 80+ candidate, it may be selected on geographic balance grounds (see #25).

| Criterion | Abbreviation | Weight | What it measures |
|---|---|---|---|
| UNESCO / authority strength | AUTH | High | Designation stack height; WH/Biosphere/Geopark/Ramsar/ICH/MPA |
| Public-domain asset strength | PD | High | Named illustrator, named expedition, confirmed active NC institution |
| Collection strength | COL | High | Depth of holdings across ≥1 active NC institution |
| Commerce strength | COM | Medium | Product types available now; ICH TC/NK paths; landscape print demand |
| Geographic balance | GEO | Medium | Region not already covered in this 25; zero-fills weighted heavily |
| ICH representation | ICH | Medium | UNESCO ICH element attached; domain (TC/NK/PA/SP/OT) |

**Score floor used:** All 25 candidates score ≥78 composite or close a documented regional zero
with no higher-scoring alternative.

**Dropped from 80+ pool:** `falkland-islands` (region covered by south-georgia),
`niagara-falls` (NA covered by hudson-river-highlands), `wood-buffalo-national-park`
(Canada boreal, weaker PD anchor), `copan-mayan-ruins` (Mesoamerica covered by chichen-itza +
tikal), `st-kilda` (UK covered by giants-causeway), `saxon-switzerland-geopark` (Geopark zero
addressed by three other Geopark places).
