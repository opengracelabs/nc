## Architecture

This portfolio defines the long-term structural ambition of Nature & Culture: **10,000 potential collections**, scaled across 2,000 global places and 100 collection families. 

Drawing from reference models like the Smithsonian, Rijksmuseum, and National Geographic, the portfolio is organized into **10 Primary Archives**. These archives bridge the Canon and Masterpiece Registries, translating high-signal individual works into comprehensive, place-based commercial and educational ecosystems.

---

## 1. Extinction Archive
**Focus:** Documenting vanished lineages and the irreversible cost of human expansion. The most powerful editorial and conservation narrative on the platform.

*   **Flagship Collections:** The Audubon Passenger Pigeon Folio, The Gould Thylacine Records, The Catesby Woodpecker Set, The Steller Sea Cow Manuscripts.
