## Asset Zero Recommendation

**ID:** NC-CMA-001  
**Name:** Cloudy Mountains  
**Creator:** Mi Youren (Chinese, 1074–1151)  
**Date:** Southern Song dynasty, dated 1130  
**CMA Accession:** 1933.220  
**Category:** Masterwork / Chinese Painting  
**Rationale:** As one of the most important Chinese landscape paintings in a North American collection, "Cloudy Mountains" is the ideal "Asset Zero" to activate NC's China place intelligence and validate CMA's direct CDN image delivery pipeline.
