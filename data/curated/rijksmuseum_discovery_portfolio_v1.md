# Rijksmuseum Discovery Portfolio v1

**Role:** Chief Curator  
**Objective:** Define the first Nature & Culture acquisition portfolio from the Rijksmuseum, identifying 50 high-fidelity, rights-cleared masterpieces.

---

## 1. Executive Summary: The Rijksmuseum Bridge

The Rijksmuseum integration provides Nature & Culture with the **"Old World Authority"** required to ground our place-centered narratives in deep history. The portfolio focuses on three core strengths: **Japanese Ukiyo-e (Kyoto)**, **18th-century Natural History (Galápagos/Reef)**, and **Global Cartography (Exploration).**
