## Asset Zero Recommendation

**ID:** NC-GETTY-001  
**Name:** Vase of Flowers  
**Creator:** Jan van Huysum (Dutch, 1682–1749)  
**Date:** 1722  
**Accession ID:** 82.PB.70  
**Category:** Natural History / European Painting  
**Rationale:** Van Huysum is the definitive exemplar of Dutch botanical still life. This masterwork fills a critical "Golden Age" natural history gap and serves as the ideal "Asset Zero" to activate Getty's Linked Art pipeline and validate high-resolution IIIF delivery for complex botanical subjects.
