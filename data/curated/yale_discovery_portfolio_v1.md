## Asset Zero Recommendation

**ID:** NC-YALE-001  
**Name:** Staffa, Fingal's Cave  
**Creator:** Joseph Mallord William Turner (British, 1775–1851)  
**Date:** 1832  
**Institution:** YCBA  
**Accession ID:** B1978.43.14  
**Category:** Turner / Maritime / Landscape  
**Rationale:** Turner's *Staffa* is a quintessential masterpiece that bridges British Landscape and Maritime Heritage. It represents the height of Romantic atmospheric painting and serves as the perfect "Asset Zero" to activate Yale's LUX-driven Linked Art pipeline and validate IIIF v3 manifest handling for the British art core.
