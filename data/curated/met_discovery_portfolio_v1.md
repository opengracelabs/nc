## Asset Zero Recommendation

**ID:** NC-MET-001  
**Name:** Under the Wave off Kanagawa (Kanagawa oki nami ura), or The Great Wave  
**Creator:** Katsushika Hokusai (Japanese, 1760–1849)  
**Date:** ca. 1830–32  
**Met Object ID:** 45434  
**Category:** Asia / Japan / Masterwork  
**Rationale:** The "Great Wave" is one of the most iconic works of art globally and a cornerstone of The Met's Japanese woodblock print collection. As a CC0 asset, it represents NC's highest-value commercial opportunity for Japan place pages and natural history/landscape illustration.
