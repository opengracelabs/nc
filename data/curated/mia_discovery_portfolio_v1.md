## 2. Asset Metadata: The Masterwork Pilots (Detail)

### NC-MIA-001: Olive Trees
*   **Creator:** Vincent van Gogh (Dutch, 1853–1890)
*   **Period:** 1889 (Saint-Rémy)
*   **Region:** Provence, France
*   **Significance:** Painted during Van Gogh's convalescence, this work represents his spiritual connection to the "enduring" nature of the Mediterranean landscape.
*   **Tourism Value:** Primary anchor for "Van Gogh's Provence" journey.
*   **Education Value:** Teaches post-impressionist landscape techniques and the cultural history of the olive tree in Europe.
*   **Product Value:** Extremely high. Global best-seller for wall art and home decor.

### NC-MIA-002: Water-Moon Guanyin
*   **Creator:** Chinese Artist (Northern Song Dynasty)
*   **Period:** 11th–12th Century
*   **Region:** Shanxi Province, China
*   **Significance:** One of the most famous wooden sculptures in the world, depicting the Bodhisattva of Compassion in a relaxed pose.
*   **Tourism Value:** Digital anchor for "The Silk Road: Buddhist Heritage."
*   **Education Value:** Explores the iconography of Guanyin and the transition of woodcarving styles in the Song Dynasty.
*   **Product Value:** High for 3D-printable miniatures and high-end photographic prints.

### NC-MIA-003: Shrine Head
*   **Creator:** Yoruba Artist (Ife, Nigeria)
*   **Period:** 12th–14th Century
*   **Region:** West Africa (Nigeria)
*   **Significance:** A technical masterpiece of terracotta sculpture, establishing the "Royal City of Ife" as a global center of classical art.
*   **Tourism Value:** Foundational anchor for the "Ancient Kingdoms of West Africa" journey.
*   **Education Value:** Critical record for teaching pre-colonial African civilization and the history of metallurgy/terracotta.
*   **Product Value:** 3D discovery pilot for "Tactile Heritage" collections.
