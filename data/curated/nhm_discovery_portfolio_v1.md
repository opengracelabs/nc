## Asset Zero Recommendation

**ID:** NC-NHM-001  
**Name:** Banks' Florilegium (Banksia serrata)  
**Creator:** Sydney Parkinson (Original sketch) / Frederick Nodder (Finished watercolour)  
**Date:** 1770–1784  
**Category:** Botanical Illustration / Pacific Heritage  
**Rationale:** The *Banks' Florilegium* represents the origin moment of European natural history illustration in the Pacific. Derived from Sydney Parkinson's field sketches during Captain Cook's first voyage (HMS Endeavour), these finished plates by Frederick Nodder (NC Priority Illustrator #6) are the definitive visual record of Australian and Pacific flora. As Asset Zero, this selection validates NHM's role as the successor to the Hans Sloane collection and anchors the platform's Asia-Pacific geographic expansion.
