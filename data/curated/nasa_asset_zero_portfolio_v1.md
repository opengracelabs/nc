## 1. Top 5 Asset Zero Packages

These five foundational assets define the NASA contribution to Nature & Culture, representing the intersection of planetary science and human heritage.

### 1.1 NC-NASA-001: The Blue Marble
*   **NASA ID:** AS17-148-22727
*   **Mission:** Apollo 17
*   **Date:** December 7, 1972
*   **Center:** Johnson Space Center (JSC)
*   **Significance:** The first high-resolution color photograph showing a fully illuminated Earth. It remains the most widely distributed image in human history.
*   **Conservation Value:** The definitive symbol of the environmental movement; established the "Whole Earth" perspective.
*   **Tourism Value:** Ultimate planetary waypoint; anchors the "Earth Discovery" landing page.
*   **Educational Value:** Teaches planetary scale, atmospheric circulation (Antarctic cyclone), and the fragility of the biosphere.
*   **Product Value:** 100/100. High-velocity wall art, apparel, and environmental advocacy merchandise.
*   **Discovery Journey:** "Planetary Nature: The Whole Earth."

### 1.2 NC-NASA-002: Earthrise
*   **NASA ID:** AS8-14-2383
*   **Mission:** Apollo 8
*   **Date:** December 24, 1968
*   **Center:** Johnson Space Center (JSC)
*   **Significance:** Captured by William Anders as the spacecraft emerged from the dark side of the Moon. Galen Rowell described it as "the most influential environmental photograph ever taken."
*   **Conservation Value:** Established Earth as an isolated "oasis" in the desert of space.
*   **Tourism Value:** Digital anchor for the "Lunar Frontier" journey.
*   **Educational Value:** Explores the "Overview Effect" and the origins of the first Earth Day.
*   **Product Value:** 98/100. Iconic prints, spiritual/philosophical giftware, and museum-grade reproductions.
*   **Discovery Journey:** "Oasis in Space: The Earthrise Moment."

### 1.3 NC-NASA-003: Pale Blue Dot
*   **NASA ID:** PIA00452
*   **Mission:** Voyager 1
*   **Date:** February 14, 1990
*   **Center:** Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL)
*   **Significance:** Earth seen as a tiny speck (0.12 pixels) from 3.7 billion miles away, famously narrated by Carl Sagan.
*   **Conservation Value:** Underscores the cosmic insignificance and unique responsibility to preserve our only home.
*   **Tourism Value:** High-authority waypoint for "Deep Time" and "Outer Reaches" discovery.
*   **Educational Value:** Philosophical and astronomical literacy; galactic scale.
*   **Product Value:** 95/100. Scientific posters, philosophical stationery, and minimalist apparel.
*   **Discovery Journey:** "The Smallest Home: A Pale Blue Dot."

### 1.4 NC-NASA-004: The Black Marble
*   **NASA ID:** GSFC_20171208_Archive_e001552
*   **Mission:** Suomi NPP / VIIRS
*   **Date:** 2012 (Composite)
*   **Center:** Goddard Space Flight Center (GSFC)
*   **Significance:** A high-definition nocturnal view of Earth's city lights, illustrating the human footprint across the planet.
*   **Conservation Value:** Visualizes light pollution, energy consumption, and urban expansion.
*   **Tourism Value:** Anchors the "Global Culture" and "City Lights" nocturnal journeys.
*   **Educational Value:** Teaches human geography, demographics, and energy systems.
*   **Product Value:** 94/100. High-tech decor, digital wallpapers, and global footprint prints.
*   **Discovery Journey:** "Human Light: The Black Marble."

### 1.5 NC-NASA-005: Yellowstone from Orbit
*   **NASA ID:** Landsat-7 ID 543 / SVS 976
*   **Mission:** Landsat 7
*   **Date:** July 13, 1999
*   **Center:** Goddard Space Flight Center (GSFC)
*   **Significance:** The first cloud-free image of the entire Yellowstone National Park, showing the 1988 fire recovery and geyser basin heat zones.
*   **Conservation Value:** Critical record of forest resilience and geothermal activity baseline.
*   **Tourism Value:** Primary orbital waypoint for the **Yellowstone Flagship**.
*   **Educational Value:** Teaches remote sensing, forest successional stages, and volcanic caldera geology.
*   **Product Value:** 90/100. Specialized "Park Edition" maps, technical prints, and outdoor/conservation gear.
*   **Discovery Journey:** "Yellowstone: The Orbital View."
