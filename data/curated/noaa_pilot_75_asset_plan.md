# NOAA Pilot 75 Asset Plan

**Project:** Nature & Culture Pilot Phase
**Institution:** NOAA
**Total Assets:** 75
**Target Ingestion Date:** Q3 2026

---

## 1. Ingestion Strategy

The Pilot 75 plan focuses on establishing a "Blue foundation" by ingesting a representative cross-section of NOAA's most iconic and scientifically significant assets. The selection is tiered to ensure that the "Asset Zero" anchors are prioritized, followed by thematic expansions that provide depth to core narratives.

## 4. Ingestion Workflow

1.  **Metadata Enrichment:** Use existing NOAA Photo Library captions as base; supplement with "Nature & Culture" significance tags.
2.  **Credit Alignment:** Ensure all assets are credited as "NOAA" or specific sub-agencies (NOAA Fisheries, NOAA Sanctuaries).
3.  **High-Res Acquisition:** Download original TIFF/High-Res JPEG versions from `photolib.noaa.gov` or `fisheries.noaa.gov`.
4.  **License Verification:** Confirm Public Domain status (98% of this list).
