## 5. Biological Intelligence (v2 Extension)

### 5.1 Botanical Species Richness
This "miraculous bouquet" features over **30 distinct species** that span multiple seasons, creating a fantasy arrangement that defies natural phenology. Key identifiable species include:
*   **The Masterpiece Row:** Cabbage Rose (*Rosa x centifolia*), Striped "Broken" Tulips (e.g., Baguette), Opium Poppies (*Papaver somniferum*), and a central Dark Blue Iris.
*   **The Canopy:** Pale Yellow Hollyhocks crown the arrangement, symbolizing the height of summer.
*   **The Understory:** Peonies, Carnations, Morning Glories (*Convolvulus tricolor*), Primroses, Hyacinths, and Apple Blossoms.
*   **Rare Accents:** Auricula, Ranunculus, Nasturtium, and a symbolic **Crown of Thorns** plant at the apex.

### 5.2 Pollinator & Fauna Relevance
Van Huysum used microscopic fauna to ground the ethereal arrangement in the cycle of life (*Vanitas*):
*   **Pollinators:** Butterflies fluttering among the upper blooms and ladybugs (Coccinellidae) nestled in the foliage.
*   **Scavengers & Guests:** Ants crawling along stems (and eating petals), common houseflies resting on leaves.
*   **Indirect Evidence:** Caterpillars are evidenced by the meticulously painted **leaf damage and holes**, showing the interaction between insects and plant life.
*   **The Nest:** A **Chaffinch nest** (*Fringilla coelebs*) containing five speckled eggs rests on the plinth, representing potential life and the fragility of the domestic sphere.
