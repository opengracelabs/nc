## 2. The Pilot 25 Launch Assets

### A. Coral Reefs
1.  **Heron Island Coral Spawning (2021)**
    *   **Source:** NOAA Photo Library / CRCP
    *   **Significance:** Peak reproductive event of the world's largest reef.
    *   **Conservation Value:** Documents reef resilience and larval health.
    *   **Tourism Value:** High aspirational value for divers.
    *   **Product Value:** High-speed macro photography prints.
2.  **"Big Momma" Giant Porites Coral**
    *   **Source:** NOAA ONMS (American Samoa)
    *   **Significance:** Ancient life (500+ years); one of the world's largest corals.
    *   **Conservation Value:** Symbol of longevity in a changing ocean.
    *   **Tourism Value:** National monument landmark.
    *   **Product Value:** Scientific "Ancient Life" series.
3.  **GBR Bleached/Healthy Contrast Diptych**
    *   **Source:** NOAA Coral Reef Watch
    *   **Significance:** Definitive climate witness for reef ecosystems.
    *   **Conservation Value:** Direct evidence of thermal stress impacts.
    *   **Tourism Value:** Awareness-driven educational value.
    *   **Product Value:** High-impact editorial/documentary asset.
4.  **Key Largo 360° Virtual Dive**
    *   **Source:** NOAA Sanctuaries (ONMS)
    *   **Significance:** Primary entry point for digital reef exploration.
    *   **Conservation Value:** Builds public empathy through immersion.
    *   **Tourism Value:** Exceptional virtual tourism potential.
    *   **Product Value:** Foundation for VR/AR platforms.
5.  **Flower Garden Banks: Brain Coral Spawning**
    *   **Source:** NOAA CRCP / ONMS
    *   **Significance:** Remote reef regeneration in the Gulf of Mexico.
    *   **Conservation Value:** Highlighting the health of protected remote reefs.
    *   **Tourism Value:** Specialty diving interest.
    *   **Product Value:** Aesthetic life-cycle imagery.
