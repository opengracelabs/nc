## Asset Zero Recommendation

**ID:** NC-NGA-001  
**Name:** The Voyage of Life: Youth  
**Creator:** Thomas Cole (American, 1801–1848)  
**Date:** 1842  
**NGA Object ID:** 1942.8.16  
**Category:** American Art / Landscape Painting  
**Rationale:** As one of the most famous and beloved series of American landscape paintings, *The Voyage of Life: Youth* is the ideal "Asset Zero." It perfectly encapsulates the Hudson River School aesthetic—a blend of awe-inspiring nature, exploration, and allegory—providing a powerful visual anchor for the NGA's world-class American Art collection.
