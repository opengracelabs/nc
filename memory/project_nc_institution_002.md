NC-INSTITUTION-002 Founding Curator Framework — DRAFT 2026-06-13, pending ratification.
File: `docs/governance/NC-INSTITUTION-002_founding_curator_framework.md`

**Core finding:** 4 roles defined separately even if one person holds all at founding: Founding Curator (permanent designation — who built the institution), Collection Curator (ongoing operational, signs certificates), Collection Editor (accountable for editorial claims), Institutional Director (signs institutional commitments).

**Minimum viable founding configuration:** One named person holds all 4 roles. Minimum public presence = one curator profile page + curatorial statement per collection + named contact email.

**The Founding Curator designation is permanent.** It records who made the founding decisions and appears in all founding-period certificates, governance documents, and the institutional history page. This is the Rijksmuseum Director model (Wim Pijbes named his CC0 decision) applied to NC.
